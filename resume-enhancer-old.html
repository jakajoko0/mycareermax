Navigating the job market? Your first challenge is to get past the Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) that recruiters use to screen resumes. Our state-of-the-art Resume Analyzer Tool is designed to give you the edge you need.

How Does It Work?
1. Upload Your Resume: Simply upload your resume in .docx format.
2. Provide Job Title (optional): Paste the job title for the position you're applying for.
3. Provide Job Description: Enter the job description for the position you're applying for.
4. Scan: Click the 'Scan' button and let our tool do the magic.

What Do You Get?
ATS-Friendliness Score: Your resume will be scored on a 1-100 scale based on how well it matches with the provided job description and ATS algorithms.

Tailored Tips: Receive personalized tips and suggestions on how to improve your resume's ATS score, from optimizing keywords to enhancing the layout.

Why Use Our Tool?
